General Information

In Emerald we train medical doctors in biomedical research to address global health challenges. This summer school aims to develop and teach fundamental skills needed for advanced Data Visualisation techniques through hands-on training. Its target audience is doctors/researchers/students who have little to no prior computational experience, and its lessons are domain specific, building on learners' existing knowledge to enable them to quickly apply skills learned to their own work/research. Participants will be encouraged to help one another and to apply what they have learned to their own research problems.

Who: The course is aimed at medical doctors/graduate students and other researchers.

Where This training will take place in Berlin. The organiserz will provide you with additional information about the venue.

When September 24 - 25, 2024
